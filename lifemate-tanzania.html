Our Story

A Legacy Built on Craftsmanship

What began as a bold vision in Lagos, Nigeria in 2002 has grown into Africa's most recognised furniture group — stretching across 10+ countries, employing over 3,000 people worldwide, and transforming more than a million African homes. Lifemate Tanzania stands proudly at the heart of this story, bringing world-class Italian-inspired design to the Tanzanian people.

The Foundation

Lifemate Group is founded in Lagos, Nigeria under the leadership of Chairman Mr. Chi Quan — with a mission to bring quality, affordable furniture to the African continent. Three core businesses are established: Furniture Products, Hardware & Machinery, and Architectural Designs.

2002

Pan-African Expansion

Lifemate opens showrooms across West and Central Africa — Ghana, Cameroon, Gabon — becoming the go-to premium furniture brand for Africa's growing middle class. With production centers in Hong Kong, Foshan, and Dalian, China, supply chain quality reaches international standards.

2010s

Arrival in Tanzania

Lifemate opens its Tanzanian flagship on Nyerere Road, Dar es Salaam — the largest furniture showroom in the country. Tanzanians immediately embrace the brand's signature blend of modern design, durability, and unmatched value for money.

2015

Digital Community & Growth

@lifematetz reaches 140,000 followers on Instagram — one of Tanzania's largest furniture brand audiences — proving that exceptional product quality and genuine customer care translate seamlessly into digital loyalty. The showroom becomes a Dar es Salaam landmark.

Today
